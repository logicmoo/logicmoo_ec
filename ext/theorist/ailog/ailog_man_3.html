3 Syntax

Atoms start with lower-case letters and can include letters, numbers and underscore ("_")

A body is either an atom, of the form alpha&beta, where alpha and beta are bodies, or of the form ~alpha, where alpha is a body.

A clause is either an atom or is a rule of the form h<-b where h is an atom (the head of the clause) and b is a body.
